About J&Z Inc.

Building Trust Through Legal Excellence.

J&Z Inc. Attorneys is a modern South African law firm committed to providing strategic legal solutions for businesses, institutions and individuals. Our approach combines legal excellence with practical commercial thinking, ensuring every client receives advice that is clear, effective and results-driven.

Our Story

Established in 2022, Joncto & Zondi Incorporated Attorneys was founded with a vision of creating a law firm built on integrity, professionalism and long-term client relationships.

Our attorneys understand that every legal matter represents an important moment in a client's life or business. We therefore provide legal advice that is strategic, commercially aware and tailored to each client's needs.

From corporate transactions and litigation to conveyancing, mining law, employment disputes and estate planning, our multidisciplinary team delivers solutions that create lasting value.
